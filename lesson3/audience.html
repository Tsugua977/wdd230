Target Audience

I want to appeal to people who are starting up their businesses. So this site will target the entrepreneurs of small businesses. I want to give opprotunities for funding and help for developing a business. These opprotunities will include demographics of the state and what businesses might be willing to help support each other. Also, many small busness user use their cell phones more than their laptops, so more focus should be put into supporting phone accesibilty.
