About Carlsbad

Located in southern California, Carlsbad is known for its oceanfront beaches and the famous kid’s park, Legoland. It’s also known for its wineries and foods. Fruit has been a great part of the history of Carlsbad. The first fruit grove to open there was an avocado orchard, which spurred more avocado groves and became a new industry there.

About Us

Me (Sydney) and my husband (Brad) have always had a passion for food. Both of our families have had roots in farming and our business reflects that. We started our business 5 years ago in Carlsbad, being supported by local organic farms. In recent years however, we’ve been able to connect with many organic farms across the California coast.

We want to give people a healthy option for their meals. With our passions of sharing fruit, we also experiment with baskets of fruit and making drinks out of a combination of fruits. If you don’t like one option on our site, there is sure to be one that catches your eye!
