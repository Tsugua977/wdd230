San Diego

San Diego is California’s second largest city, supporting 1.3 million people in the city. It’s known for its expansive beaches, outdoor opportunities, ideal climate, and family place activities.

The farming culture is big, as many are growing flowers, fruit, and other kinds of produce. Many of the organic farms from San Diego support us with their fruit. We often rely upon them for oranges, lemons, and limes.

Los Angeles

Los Angeles is the biggest city in California with a population of 3.9 million. It is the state’s cultural, financial, and commercial center. The movie industry is prevalent here, as Hollywood’s beginnings started in here.

Included in Los Angeles’s commercial industry is farming. Even though many of the farms are commercial, there are still some small organic farms on the city’s edge. They support Bountiful Foods with dragon fruit, apples, and peaches.
